By Train :

There is about 6 direct trains daily from Paris "Montparnasse" to La Rochelle and the average price for a ticket is $50 (cheaper if bought in advance).

Click here to make a train reservation

Once in La Rochelle you will need to take the bus to the island. The bus will be in front of the train station and is called "Les Mouettes". The price of a ticket from La Rochelle to Ars-en-Re will be between 4 and 6 euros.


From England or Ireland :

There is an International airport in La Rochelle with regular connections to different cities in England and Ireland.
